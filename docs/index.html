Flicks With Friends

PICK TOGETHER. WATCH TOGETHER.

Can't decide what to watch? Flicks With Friends lets your group swipe on movies and shows, then reveals what everyone agrees on. No more endless scrolling or debates — just matches you'll all enjoy.

How It Works

1
Create or Join Start a session and share the code with friends, or join one with a code you've received.
2
Swipe on Movies & Shows Everyone swipes right on titles they'd watch and left on ones they'd skip.
3
See Your Matches When everyone's done, see the titles your group all agreed on. Movie night, decided.

FAQ

Do I need to create an account?
No. Just pick a nickname and an avatar to get started. No signup or login required.
How long do sessions last?
Sessions automatically expire after 24 hours. You can start a new one anytime.
How many people can join a session?
Up to 10 people can join a single session.
What data do you collect?
Only your chosen nickname, avatar, and swipe selections — all tied to a temporary session, not a personal account. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Is it free?
Yes, Flicks With Friends is completely free to use.
