Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Flicks With Friends ("the App") is designed to help groups decide what to watch together. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account, sign up, or log in. There is no email, password, or personal identity information collected at any point.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect only the following:

  • Nickname — the display name you choose when joining a session.
  • Avatar selection — the avatar you pick from the available options.
  • Swipe selections — your right/left swipe choices on movies and shows within a session.

All of this data is tied to a temporary session, not to any personal account or device identifier.

How Data Is Stored

Session data (nicknames, avatars, and swipe selections) is stored in Supabase, a cloud database service. Sessions automatically expire and are deleted after 24 hours. We do not retain your data beyond that window.

Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash and error reports. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Sentry does not collect personal information, device identifiers, or any data that can be used to identify you individually. No other analytics or tracking tools are used in the App.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose. Data is only used to operate the App's core session functionality and to fix bugs via anonymous crash reports.

No Tracking or Analytics

The App does not use advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or any technology that tracks your behavior across apps or websites. The only external service is Sentry for error reporting.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected from any user, there is no children's data at risk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at flickswithfriends@gmail.com.
